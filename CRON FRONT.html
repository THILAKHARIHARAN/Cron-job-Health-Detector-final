SRE Help Guide & Documentation

Quick reference manual for the Cron-Job Observability Engine.

1. System Onboarding & Tour

If you are a new user, click the Interactive Tour button to start a guided walkthrough. It highlights key sections like KPIs, timeline graphs, registered schedules, and telemetry logs while dimming the background.

2. Registering Cron Subprocesses

Click + Register Job in the configured schedules panel. Provide the job name, assigned operator, cron schedule expression (e.g. */10 * * * *), and the exact CLI execution command. Job type and category are automatically defaulted to 'other' behind the scenes to keep options simple.

3. Telemetry Stream Inspections

When a subprocess executes (either scheduled, manually triggered, or simulated), the engine captures console stdout/stderr streams. Under the logbook table, click Inspect Logs or Inspect & AI Audit on any row to view full logs and AI-generated SRE diagnostics.

4. Live SRE Controls

Use the sticky utility control bar at the bottom to perform operations:

  • Simulate Run: Generates random subprocess runs for test purposes.
  • Switch Tone: Toggles automated SRE diagnostics between reassuring and cautious modes.
  • Prune Logs: Purges execution history.
  • Reload: Refreshes the dashboard data.
